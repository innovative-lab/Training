The App

The Medical Alert application is used to send an emergency message to the medical team of Mintdtree Kalinga, when a Campus Mind need their help immediately. It provides the location details of the person who requested for help.

Details

The application will be used by the Campus Minds in Kalinga, Mindtree. It will be connected with admin team of Medical group at Kalinga Orchard team, Campus Minds and the people inside Kalinga campus can download this app on their mobiles and can be used. Once the apllication is download in their mobile, we need to sign-in, the details of the person will be stored, during the time of any help, they can send a message with their details and location tapped to the medical team, so that they can help whenever needed.
